My name is Joe Sak, I'm a 6th year senior at Michigan State University.
I've been studying Game Design for 3 years now, going on 4. I searched for a few years trying to find a major that interested me. My first 5 semesters of college were in 5 different majors. Fifth time is apparently a charm however seeing how I have settled in to Media and Information. I've since held three jobs in the field of game design, and have distinguished myself through:

  • Games for Entertainment and Learning(GEL) Lab employee
  • Empathetic Games Program employee
  • Indiginous Peoples Games Project employee
  • Developing and Publishing 2 games
  • Official Steam Partner

Combining this with 20+ years experience of playing board and video games, creating my own video games on my calculator in high school, and the countless homebrew board games I've created, I believe I am in the right place for me.
